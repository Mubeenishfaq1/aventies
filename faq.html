FAQ

Quick Answers, Clear Solutions!.
Can I Get Approved with $0 Down?

Yes! At Aventis we work with a wide range of lenders who offer $0 down financing options for qualified applicants. Even if you have bad credit or no credit, we strive to find flexible solutions that fit your budget. Approval is based on factors like income, credit history, and lender requirements, but many of our customers drive away with little to no money down.


Is My Information Safe and Secure?

Absolutely. At Aventis protecting your personal information is our top priority. Our website and application process use industry-leading encryption and security measures to keep your data private and secure, and we never run a credit check without your permission. We only work with trusted lenders and dealerships, ensuring a safe and transparent financing experience from start to finish. Apply with confidence, knowing your information is in safe hands.


How Do I Get Started?

Simply fill out our online application, and we'll connect you with financing options tailored to your needs. It's fast, secure, and hassle-free! Still have questions? Contact us today, and our team will be happy to help!


What Kind of Vehicles Can I Finance?

We work with trusted dealerships offering a wide selection of new and used cars, trucks, SUVs, and more. Once approved, you can choose from vehicles that match your budget and financing terms.
